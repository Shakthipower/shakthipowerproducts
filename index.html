About Us

Shakthi Power Products (SPP) is a leading manufacturer and solution provider for energy-efficient power systems. We specialize in developing innovative electrical panels such as APFC, MCC, DB, and metering panels to reduce energy costs and enhance performance.

With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we support businesses across industries in optimizing their power usage, achieving compliance, and minimizing EB penalties through smart solutions.
